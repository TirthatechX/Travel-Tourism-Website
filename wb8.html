These are only 30% of world's tourist destinations. The rest 70% are -

Europe -

France:
Eiffel Tower, Louvre Museum, and charming cafes.
Netherlands:
Amsterdam's canals and museums, windmill-dotted village of Kinderdijk, Rotterdam's modern architecture.
Spain:
Barcelona's Gaudí’s architecture and beaches, Madrid's museums and nightlife.

Asia -

Japan:
Tokyo’s futuristic skyline and Kyoto’s ancient temples.
China:
The Great Wall, Forbidden City, and vibrant Shanghai.
Thailand:
Bangkok’s temples and beaches of Phuket.
India:
The Taj Mahal, Rajasthan’s palaces, Goa’s beaches and Ladakh-Kashmir region.
Indonesia:
Bali’s tropical resorts and spiritual retreats.
UAE:
Dubai's skyline, Abu Dhabi's Sheikh Zayed Grand Mosque and Sharjah's rich heritage.

Africa -

Egypt:
Pyramids of Giza and the Nile River.
Morocco:
Marrakech’s souks and Atlas Mountains.
South Africa:
Cape Town, safaris in Kruger National Park.
Tanzania:
Serengeti and Mount Kilimanjaro treks.

Central and South America -

Mexico:
Chichen Itza, Cancun, and vibrant festivals.
Brazil:
Rio de Janeiro’s Carnival, Christ the Redeemer.
Peru:
Machu Picchu and the Amazon rainforest.
Argentina:
Buenos Aires tango shows and Patagonia wilderness.
Columbia:
Bogotá's vibrant culture, Cartagena's colonial architecture, Santa Marta's beautiful beaches.
